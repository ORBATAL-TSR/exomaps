# Colony Systems & Life Support

## Colony Types
- Surface bases: domes, underground, modular habitats
- Orbital stations: ring habitats, O'Neill cylinders, asteroid bases
- Mobile colonies: ships, generation vessels, nomad fleets

## Life Support
- Oxygen, water, food production and recycling
- Waste management, environmental control
- Radiation shielding, temperature regulation
- Population dynamics: growth, morale, health

## Colony Management
- Resource allocation: mining, manufacturing, trade
- Expansion: new modules, population growth, terraforming
- Governance: player-driven policies, AI management, events
- Emergency response: disasters, disease, supply shortages

## Colony Growth
- Research: science labs, technological breakthroughs
- Economy: local markets, trade, production chains
- Social: morale, culture, education, entertainment
