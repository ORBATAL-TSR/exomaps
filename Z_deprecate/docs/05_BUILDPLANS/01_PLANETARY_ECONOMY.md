# Planetary Economy & Resource Modeling

## Mineral Value System
- Procedural generation of mineral deposits (iron, nickel, rare earths, uranium, platinum, volatiles, etc.)
- Deposits mapped spatially: surface, subsurface, oceanic, atmospheric
- Extraction difficulty: geology, depth, planetary conditions
- Dynamic market: Prices fluctuate based on supply, demand, extraction cost, accessibility
- Resource rarity and technological breakthroughs affect value

## Planet Suitability & Life Potential
- Habitability index: atmosphere, temperature, gravity, radiation, water, biosignature presence
- Terraforming potential: atmosphere modification, temperature control, biosphere seeding
- Life support requirements: oxygen, water, food, temperature, radiation shielding
- Colony sustainability: resource consumption, recycling, environmental hazards

## Economy Simulation
- Local planetary markets, interstellar trade routes
- Dynamic pricing, supply/demand, player-driven arbitrage
- Resource scarcity, technological breakthroughs, events
- Production chains: Mining → Refining → Manufacturing → Construction
- Shipyards, colony factories, research labs
